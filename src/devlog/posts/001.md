SUBJECT: Environmental Audio Synthesis
LOCATION: Whitechapel District (Simulation)

We have successfully captured the acoustic signature of heavy rainfall on cobblestone. The initial synthesis lacked 'weight', sounding more like static than precipitation.

ADJUSTMENTS MADE:

- Added randomized pitch modulation to droplet impacts based on surface material (Cobble vs. Wood vs. Flesh).
- Implemented spatial occlusion for overhangs and archways.
- Recorded foley samples using wet wool and slate tiles.
